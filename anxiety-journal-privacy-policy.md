## Summary
Anxiety Journal stores your entries only on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or sell your data.

**Information you provide**
- Episode logs such as time, intensity, duration, triggers, and notes.

**Information collected automatically**

## How We Use Information
- To display and manage your episode history inside the app.
- To export your data when you choose to share it.

## Data Storage and Sharing
- Your data is stored locally on your device using on‑device storage.
- We do not upload your data to any server.
- If you export data (e.g., CSV/PDF), it is shared only through the system share sheet you control.

## Third‑Party Services
- The app does not use third‑party analytics, advertising, or crash reporting services.

## Data Retention
- Your data stays on your device until you delete it or uninstall the app.

## Security
- We rely on your device’s operating system protections. No method of storage is 100% secure, but we do not transmit your data off device.

## Children’s Privacy
